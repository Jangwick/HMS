Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Password resets must be coordinated through your Department Head and the HR3 Subsystem (Workforce Operations). After verification, an administrator will issue a temporary token.

Why can't I access another department's hub?

HMS enforces a strict isolation policy (HMS Fundamentals Policy) to protect sensitive data. You only have access to the subsystem assigned to your specific role.

System is running slowly, what should I do?

Ensure you are using a modern browser (Chrome/Edge) and clear your browser cache. If performance persists within your local network, contact the IT Infrastructure team at EXT 1200.
