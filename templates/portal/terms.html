Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I forget to log out?

For security compliance with the National Healthcare Data Protection Act, the system will automatically terminate your session after 30 minutes of inactivity. However, you remain responsible for any actions taken while your session is active.

Can I use the system on my personal mobile device?

HMS is accessible via mobile browsers for viewing, but certain administrative and financial actions are restricted to hospital-authorized hardware to ensure encrypted endpoint security.

Are my private searches within the system monitored?

Yes. In accordance with the Digital Medical Privacy Law, all system interactions, including search queries and record views, are indexed in the core audit logs for patient safety and legal auditing.
