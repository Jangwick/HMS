# HMS Subsystem Questionnaires

Purpose: Gather concise, formal, and surface-level requirements for each subsystem so answers can be converted into system features per department and per hub.

Instructions: For each subsystem below, answer the 10 questions succinctly. If the subsystem will operate across multiple hubs, answer each question per hub or denote "All hubs" where applicable.

---

1. What is the primary objective of the Talent Acquisition subsystem?
2. Who are the primary users and roles for this subsystem?
3. What are the top three tasks performed daily or weekly?
4. What approvals or decision points are required in the hiring workflow?
5. What core data entities must be captured and stored?

8. What reports, KPIs, or dashboards are needed to measure success?
9. Which events should trigger notifications, and by what channels?
10. What are the acceptance criteria and priority level for this subsystem?
